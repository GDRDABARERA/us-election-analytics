        Mapping Twitter
        Interaction

        Supporter numbers aren’t everything. There’s a complex geography to how Twitter communities interact, especially around an election. In the following community graph, each node is a tweep and each link is interactions (e.g. retweets). Here, the larger nodes represent accounts getting lots of attention - lots of likes, lots of retweets. Their color defines who they vote for, or at least who they tweet about the most.
